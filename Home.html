What is New Year?

New Year is the time or day at which a new calendar year begins and the calendar's year count increments by one. Many cultures celebrate the event in some manner.

How is New Year celebrated

New Year's is often marked by fireworks, parades, and reflection upon the last year while looking ahead to a New Year of future possibilities. Many people celebrate the holiday in the company of loved ones, observing traditions meant to bring luck and success in the upcoming year.

Who first celebrated New Year?

The Pacific island of Tonga is first to ring in the New Year and celebrated at 10am GMT on December 31 - making the tiny island nation the first to head into a fresh year.
